Project 1

MAXX-Power Website Redesign

My first professional project, both in my career and in my internship with Richmond's MAXX-Potential, was the task of redesigning a website to fit to specific goals within a two week time period. The team I was apart of consisted of myself and three others, and the team's Scrum Master. The website prior to our team's redeisgn looked like this:

Previous Next

After studying what kind of sprint would be effective for our group and working situation, I deployed a week-long sprint that included both small and large tasks that varied from creating a customized logo, to reworking entire CSS elements. We used Trello, along with daily meetings, to create a solidified web of communication among group members over the remaining sprint. At the end of the aforementioned sprint, our team completed a redesign of the MAXXpower.tech website, as seen in the carousel below:

Previous Next

I learned many valuable lessons from this first project, such as a better grasp of how to evaluate what goals you set for a group, how to communicate with a group in a clear and efficent manner, and it taught me the general mindset I would need to take for the rest of the projects I would complete with MAXX-Potential.

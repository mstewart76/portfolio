Project 3

Password Management System

My third and final rotation within the internship offered by MAXX-Potential, I stepped into a leadership position for the second time in our Security rotation. Our goal for this rotation is to decide on, write policy on, and theoretically implement a password management system. Our first task was to explore the market for software that could perform password management with sixty employees, and still be cost effective for the company.

We combed through multiple pieces of software, and three of those options that we looked at are scrolling below.

Previous Next

Out of the three programs mentioned, our team decided to with Keeper, due to it's low cost and coverage of features that we saw as required. As soon as we recognized what program we were going to use, we immediately started writing policy on how to use the program to success. We used multiple password strength testing websites with our example passwords to test the strength of the policy, and after testing, our group got to a comfortable point with our policy strength.

While policy was being researched and written, the rest of our work went towards understanding how to accustom employees to the software and how to fully understand it ourselves. After a few days of work, we began preparing our presentation and Executive Summary, both of which can be found below.
